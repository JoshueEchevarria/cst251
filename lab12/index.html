Lab Purpose

This lab we worked with centering different elements, text and images. We worked in CSS to do those things, we learned centering is not the easiest but it can be done. Some things we had to do in this lab were:
Style all your divs so they are 30% wide.
Style your floated div so it floats left or right.
Style your center-horz div so the text inside is centered.
Style your center-img div so your image is centered horizontally.
Style your center-both div so that the image is centered both vertically and horizontally.
